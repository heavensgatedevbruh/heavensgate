About Heaven's Gate

🎮 The Game

Heaven's Gate is a blockchain-powered flappy bird game where players compete for real prizes. Connect your Solana wallet, pay the entry fee, and flap your way to victory!

💰 Prize Pool

Every game contributes to the prize pool. The top 3 players take home 50%, 30%, and 20% of the total pool respectively.

🔗 Blockchain

Built on Solana for fast, low-cost transactions. All scores and payouts are verified on-chain for complete transparency.
